LEVEL LEVELS

There are 199 mortal and 7 immortal levels available on Archipelago. In order of rank from lowest to highest, the immortal levels are Avatar, Builder, Bodhisattva, Daemon, Djinn, Overlord, and Implementor.

To see how much experience points is required to attain certain levels, type 'level {min_level} {max_level}'. 

e.g.,
:level 3 6

[  3]      3000-6000      :
[  4]      6000-12000     :
[  5]     12000-25000     :
[  6]     25000-45000     :
