Finding that certain someone: WHERE

Tells you the location of a mobs, a player or an object.

Mortals can only WHERE to find what other players are currently in the zoen they are in,

Immortals may type WHERE without arguments to see where everybody is.

Ponder has coded WHERE to work also with a second keyword.

: where
Shows where all the players are.

: where torch
Shows where all all the torches are.

: where iron sword
Shows where all the iron swords are.

: where dragon
Shows where all the dragons are.

: where wivernhoe guard
Shows where all the Wivernhoe guards are.
