All for one and one for all: ASSIST 
Usage: ASSIST {person in need}
If Max is being beaten senseless by a hemogoblin and you feel brave enough to help him, type ASSIST MAX to hit the hemogoblin. Note that the hemogoblin will keep hitting Max; assist only makes you start to fight whomever you're trying to assist.

: assist max

See also: RESCUE, FLEE
