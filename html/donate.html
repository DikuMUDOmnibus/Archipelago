DONATE

This command takes an object that you no longer want and deposits it in the donation shop in either Lamman, Abernach or Wivernhoe, to assist newbies in getting started. There is a small chance that an item will get junked when it is donated. Money donated goes to the poor box just inside the Temple of Small Gods in Lamman.

donate {object}
donate {number} coins

: donate shield
: donate all.bread
: donate 500 coins

See also: JUNK DONATION
