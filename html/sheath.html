Using Scabbards: DRAW & SHEATHE

Cool commands... 'nuff said.

However, if you are really curious... When wearing a scabbard and a weapon, such as a sword, you can SHEATHE a wielded sword. This places the blade in the scabbard. If you wish to fight something or someone, DRAW your weapon. By doing so, you will instantly wield your weapon. Also, if you are jumped by an aggresive monster or person, you will automatically draw your weapon and begin to fight. Note: these two commands only work if you are wearing or carrying a scabbard.
