Keeping things secure: LOCK and UNLOCK

To keep things such as doors and chests secure from thievery, they are locked. In fact, any number of portals and containers can be locked. Even things such as rings, tables, and desks can be locked. In order to open locked portals and containers, you will need the proper key.

{lock|unlock} {portal|container} [direction]

: lock door
: unlock door south

: lock chest
: unlock chest

See also: OPEN, CLOSE, PICKLOCK
