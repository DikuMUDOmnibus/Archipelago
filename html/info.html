INFO NEWS POLICY CREDITS VERSION

These commands show you important information about Archipelago. It is a good idea to read them every once in a while. 

info     - useful information about the game for beginners
news     - what is abuzz on Archipelago
policy   - policies on topics like playerkilling
credits  - the people who made Archipelago possible!
version  - displays the current Archipelago version number
