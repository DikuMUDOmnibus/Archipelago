ANSI Color Codes

ANSI color %codes can be included in prompts and titles to help clarify or accentuate information. The %codes available are:

  • %b - bold
  • %0 - color normal (remember to turn color back to normal)
  • %1 - red
  • %2 - green
  • %3 - yellow
  • %4 - blue
  • %5 - magenta (reddish-purple)
  • %6 - cyan (dark blue)
  • %7 - white
