Banks: DEPOSIT, WITHDRAW, and BALANCE

Money has physical weight, the more money you carry, the more it will weight you down. Banks help alleviate this problem by storing your money for you. Not only can banks store money, but also equipment for a small surcharge.

There are known to be banks located in Lamman, Abernach, and Wivernhoe.

You can DEPOSIT, WITHDRAW, as well as check your BALANCE at a bank.
