Expressing your true self: EMOTE

Show people what you're feeling. The shorthand is ":".

"emote {string}" will display your name, then the string. For example, if your name is "Neelum",

: emote has a shifty look on his face and looks ready for trouble.

will display:

Neelum has a shifty look on his face and looks ready for trouble.
