Repairing and Resizing Equipment

Items that no longer perform as they should, are worn out, or broken, can be fixed at a craftsperson. Not all items can be fixed and resized by any craftsperson, you have to find the right one who can do the type of work you want done for the item. There are armors, weapons, treasures, worn, and other object types.
